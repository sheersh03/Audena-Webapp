Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as a "minute"? expand_more
Minutes are calculated based on the total duration of active voice processing during a call. We offer sub-second rounding for the most accurate billing possible.
Can I switch plans mid-month? expand_more
Yes, you can upgrade at any time. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.
Do you offer discounts for non-profits? expand_more
We certainly do! Verified non-profit organizations are eligible for a 25% discount on all monthly plans. Contact our sales team to apply.
Is there an implementation fee? expand_more
Starter and Growth plans have no setup fees. Enterprise plans may include a one-time implementation and onboarding fee depending on the complexity of your requirements.
